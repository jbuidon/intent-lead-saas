👥 Facebook Groups — scan posts & comments from groups you joined

  1. Go to developers.facebook.com → Create App → Business
  2. Add Facebook Login as a product
  3. Open Graph API Explorer → Generate Access Token → add permission: groups_access_member_info
  4. Paste the token in ⚙ Settings → Facebook Token

📘 Facebook Pages — scan any public page for interested commenters

  1. Uses the same Facebook token from Settings — no extra setup needed
  2. Works on any public Facebook page — competitor pages, niche pages, community pages
  3. Finds people commenting "I'm interested", "how much?", "I need this" on page posts

📸 Instagram — scan comments on your own posts for interested people

  1. Uses your Facebook token — but your Instagram account must be a Business or Creator account
  2. Link your Instagram to your Facebook Page: Facebook Page → Settings → Instagram
  3. Finds commenters saying "interested", "how much?", "I need this" on your posts
  4. Each lead shows the person's @username and a direct link to their Instagram profile
Automatically finds all Instagram accounts linked to your token
